what is EQ and why is it so important?

what is EQ?

EQ is emotional intelligence. It is the perception, evaluation and use of emotions to increase self-awareness and build strong constructive connections with oneself and others. A hierarchy of EQ skills can be understood as follows (from basic to complex):

  1. Perception of emotions
  2. Reasoning with emotions
  3. Understanding emotions
  4. Managing emotions

Emotional intelligence can be broken down into four key attributes:

  1. Self-management: healthily managing emotions and behaviours, taking initiative, upholding commitments and being adaptable
  2. Self-awareness: recognizing one's own strengths and limitations, understanding the effects of ones' emotions on ones' behaviour and self-confidence
  3. Social awareness: having empathy in understanding others needs, emotions and concerns, reading emotional queues adeptly, social comfort and recognition of group dynamics
  4. Relationship management: skill in developing and maintaining relationships, clear communication, ability to work well with, inspire and influence others, and conflict management
